Question 1: Questions regarding this article: https://www.troyhunt.com/the-red-cross-blood-service-australias-largest-ever-leak-of-personal-data/

  1. Which Red Cross Blood Service information assets were involved (what sort of information and how much of it)?
    • 1.74gb of doner information, including names, physical addresses, blood types, email addresses and dates of birth.
  2. What is the data state of the compromised information: is the data in transmission, in storage or being processed?
    • In storage
  3. Which of the following security goals were compromised? Explain your answer.
    • Confidientiality
  4. Would this be considered an attack? Explain your answer, considering both the threat and vulnerability.
    • No, since the data was publically available.
  5. After this incident, how could the compromised information be used by others outside of the company in subsequent events?
    • Blackmail, social engineering, identity theft, extortion
  6. Could this incident have been prevented? Would control measures involve the use of technology, policy and procedures, or education/training/awareness? Explain your answer (it can be more than one of these things).
    • Encryption, staff training.
